As 2024 nears, I wanted to look at the applications I use every day. Am I using the best options that support my personal and professional goals? Am I spending too much money on silly subscriptions? What can I bring in-house? Is this all over-hyped productivity crap? Oof.

Shortly after creating my omg.lol account, I came across Robb Knight's post - Hemispheric Views - App Defaults . Inspired by the countless folks who contributed, I figured I would throw my hat in the ring for an introductory Weblog post.

I will be reviewing and revising this list over 2024. What will I be using next year? Will my goals even be the same? I'm excited about the idea of personal change-logs, and this seems like a great place to start.

My List

  • 📨 Mail Client - Spark Mail Classic - Classic has everything I need, the "All New" Spark is just too busy and hard for me to manage
  • 📮 Mail Server - Gmail
  • 📝 Notes - Bear - Quick, short format notes
  • ✅ To-Do - Todoist - Integrations with Fantastical are unbeatable for productivity
  • 🟦 Photo Management - Photos.app - I take so few, the free tier will last for ages
  • 📆 Calendar - Fantastical - Simple with a great feature-set
  • 📁 Cloud File Storage - iCloud, Google Drive
  • 📖 RSS - Reeder - It's clean, basic, and to the point
  • 🙍🏻‍♂️ Contacts - Contacts.app
  • 🌐 Browser - Firefox - After Google took away ad-block, I moved to Firefox and haven't looked back
  • 💬 Chat - Discord, Matrix - Recently discovered Matrix and hoping to find more folks on there
  • 🔖 Bookmarks - Raindrop.io - Looking to move to something open source/self-hosted
  • 📑 Read It Later - Reeder - See above
  • 📜 Word Processing - Obsidian - *Once I tried it, I was hooked
  • 📈 Spreadsheets - Excel/Sheets
  • 📊 Presentations - Slides - Thinking about checking out iAPresenter. Really don't make presentations too often, which is nice.
  • 💰 Budgeting / Finance - Copilot - Not loving it, not hating it.
  • 🎧 Music - Spotify/Soundcloud
  • 🎤 Podcasts - Spotify - Hoping to check out other options as I intend to get more into listening in 2024
  • 🐘 Mastodon Client - Ivory - Shoutout Tapbots
  • 🖥 Terminal - Tabby
  • 🧰 Code Editor - Visual Studio Code
  • 🚀 Launcher - Raycast
