App Defaults

These are the apps that I use across my Apple ecosystem.

Inspired by Hemispheric Views 097 - Duel of the Defaults! and this post by Robb Knight

  • Mail Client: Apple Mail
  • Mail Service: Gmail
  • Cloud File Storage: iCloud
  • Notes: Apple Notes / Tot
  • To-Do: Due
  • Photo Shooting: iOS Camera
  • Photo Library: iCloud
  • Photo Management: Photos
  • Photo Editing: Photomator
  • Calendar: Fantastical
  • RSS Client: Unread and Reeder
  • Browser: Arc on Mac, Safari on iPhone and iPad
  • Chat: iMessage / Telegram / Messenger
  • Bookmarks: Safari
  • Read It Later: GoodLinks
  • Word Processing: Word
  • Spreadsheets: Excel
  • Presentations: PowerPoint
  • Personal Finance: Starling Bank app
  • News: Apple News / BBC News
  • Music: Apple Music
  • Podcasts: PocketCasts
  • Passwords: 1Password
  • Delivery Tracking: Parcel
  • Video Editing: LumaFusion
  • Weather: Mercury Weather
  • Mastodon: Ivory
  • Screenshots: CleanShot X
  • TV/Movie Tracking: TV Forecast / Sequel
