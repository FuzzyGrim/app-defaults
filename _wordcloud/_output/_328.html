There seems an interest in the hardware and software people are using, so much so that there is a dedicated “uses” movement where people document what they use. Well, this is my list of some of the technology I use…

Hardware

I work on either a 2017 15" Intel Apple MacBook Pro or a 2021 14" M1 Pro MacBook Pro. I use a Keychron K2 wireless mechanical keyboard and a Logitech MX Master 3 mouse. I have a Griffin Elevator laptop stand and use LG 27UK850 27" Monitor. I keep things connected using a Elgato Thunderbolt 3 Dock. Backups are handled by a 2TB Seagate external hard-drive. I use a WiiM Amp to power my Cambridge Audio Minx Min22 speakers and subwoofer.

In my office, I sit on a Herman Miller Aero chair and use a Secretlab MAGNUS Pro sit/stand desk. I have a set of Nanoleaf panels to keep me illuminated and add a bit of colour. I use a Netatmo weatherstation to monitor the temperature, humidity and air quality.

My house WiFi is powered by a Nighthawk X6 AC3200 R8000 router. I have a TP-LINK Powerline adapter to provide Internet and WiFi to other areas. I use Philips Hue lighting throughout my home. I talk to my house with a couple of Google Home Minis and pictures with a Google Nest Hub.

Away from my desk, I tap an iPhone 12 Pro, wear an Apple Watch Series 6 to track my fitness and a pair of Apple AirPods 3 to listen to music. I fly a DJI Spark drone to capture the world from interesting angles. When cycling on my Orbea Wild FS electric mountain bike I use a Garmin Edge 530 to record my rides.

I have Sony Bravia 43" television and Sony STRDN1060 receiver which powers a Monitor Audio MASS 5.1 system including an upgraded Radius 390 subwoofer. I use a Playstation 3 to play games.

Software

I run macOS. My web browser of choice is Firefox. To keep me productive I use Alfred to launch applications and general shortcuts. I use Spotify to listen to music. Slack is the tool I use to talk to clever folk. I keep track on my favourite websites via RSS using Feedly. I keep notes in Obsidian.

Web Development

I use Visual Studio Code for my web development. I use the Dark+ theme with the Menlo font. I use Sequel Pro or Sequel Ace to access databases. For my termnial I use iTerm2 with Oh My Zsh and the Solarized Dark theme.
