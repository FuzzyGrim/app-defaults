Since November, I’ve watched with interest as hundreds of bloggers have listed their default apps. I’m an early adopter and opinionated about the tools I use, so I thought I’d join in by listing my own. I’m using the same categories as Carl Barenbrug did here. This is an update of a post I made almost exactly four years ago.

  • Mail server: Google Workspace
  • Mail client: Mimestream
  • Writing (notes, longform, everything): Obsidian
  • Photography: Obscura
  • Photo management: Apple Photos, Darkroom
  • Calendar: Cron
  • Cloud file storage: Sync.com
  • RSS: Feedbin (with Reeder on iOS)
  • Contacts: Apple Contacts
  • Launcher: Alfred 5
  • Browser: Safari
  • Chat: Apple Messages, Signal, WhatsApp
  • Bookmarks: Eagle, Are.na
  • Read It Later: Readwise Reader (and Matter, for podcast transcripts)
  • Collaborative Word Processing: Google Docs, Microsoft Word
  • Spreadsheets: Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets
  • Shopping Lists: Apple Reminders
  • Budgeting: Notion
  • News: Feedbin, Feeeed
  • Music: Apple Music, MP3s, Last.fm
  • Podcasts: Pocket Casts
  • Password Management: 1Password
  • VPN: Mullvad
  • Code Editor: Nova
  • Maps: Apple Maps
  • AI Assistant: Not yet
