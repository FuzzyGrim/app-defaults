I’m captivated by small online trends, like the recent wave of sharing the default apps people use every day.

It started with Hemispheric Views’ entertaining “Duel of the Defaults!” podcast episode and was further popularized by Robb Knight’s excellent initiative to collect app defaults on his website https://defaults.rknight.me/. It’s the software world’s version of sharing desk setups1, and I can’t get enough of it!

It’s nothing big, but you might discover some new apps or workflows that you haven’t heard of before. I know I did when I was reading through other people’s app defaults.
