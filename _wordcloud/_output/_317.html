App Defaults

I’m way overdue to share my list of App Defaults. A bunch of really great people have already shared theirs, and it’s been super cool to see what other folks use (and, by extension, how they work).

I picked up the domain uses.lol a while back, thinking I might make another little omg.lol thing for this very purpose, but haven’t had a chance to do anything with it yet. (No surprise there, I suppose!)

Anyway, here goes:

  • Email: Fastmail
    • I use the web client as a “standalone” app with Safari’s “Add to Dock”, and the iOS app on my phone.
  • Notes/To-Do: Obsidian
    • No plugins! Stock app only.
  • Calendar: Calendar.app
  • Contacts: Contacts.app
  • Camera: Camera.app
  • Photo Management: Photos.app
  • File Storage: iCloud & Synology
  • RSS: Feedly
  • Browser: Safari
  • Mastodon: Ivory
    • I also really enjoy using the regular Mastodon web client, which is very nicely designed.
  • Chat: Discord, irssi, Element, Monal
  • Calculator: Calculator
  • Word Processing: Pages
  • Spreadsheet: Numbers
  • Presentations: Keynote
  • Budgeting: YNAB
  • News: Apple News+
  • Music: Apple Music
  • Movies: Plex
  • Podcasts: Apple Podcasts
  • Password Management: 1Password
  • Terminal: Terminal.app, iTerm2
  • Code Editor: Nova
