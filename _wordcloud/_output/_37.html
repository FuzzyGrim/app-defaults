Thanks to Andrea I’ve discovered this small, little and (for me) extremely funny web experiment. Publishing and sharing one’s app defaults list.

Here’s mine (as of 7 nov. 2023).

  • 📨 Mail Client: Thunderbird and Apple Mail (Outlook since a couple of months ago since my employee as moved to M365);
  • 📮 Mail Server: Google Workspace with (free) custom domain (while it lasts), Gmail, iCloud;
  • 📝 Notes: Notes.app and my beloved SimpleNote
  • ✅ To-Do: Reminders.app and Things (which I use less than I’d like to)
  • 📷 iPhone Photo Shooting: iOS Camera
  • 🟦 Photo Management: Photos.app
  • 📆 Calendar: Calendar.app
  • 📁 Cloud File Storage: Dropbox, iCloud Drive, NextCloud which I’m slowly moving to OneDrive (as for Outlook above);
  • 📖 RSS: Feedly (free plan) with Reeder
  • 🙍🏻‍♂️ Contacts: Contacts.app
  • 🌐 Browser: Firefox, Safari and Edge/Chrome just for work’s issues;
  • 💬 Chat: Discord, Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp (and also Instagram private messages), Skype for work. Teams sometimes but I try to avoid it;
  • 🔖 Bookmarks: Firefox locally with a periodic, manual, sync over iCloud. I do have a Pinboard account (which I also love)
  • 📑 Read It Later: Pinboard and a private email address;
  • 📜 Word Processing: Byword, Bbedit, MacDown;
  • 📈 Spreadsheets – 📊 Presentations: I avoid them whenever I can;
  • 🛒 Shopping Lists: Reminders.app
  • 🍴 Meal Planning: n/a
  • 💰 Budgeting and Personal Finance: Money Pro;
  • 📰 News: Mastodon, my network of friends;
  • 🎵 Music: Spotify (family plan) and also Apple Music
  • 🎤 Podcasts: Podcast.app (tried Marco Arment’s Overcast … but I do listen very few podcasts so I’ve stopped using it);
  • 🔐 Password Management: 1Password Family, a shared Keepass for work;
  • 👾 Terminal: Terminal.app … tried to use iTerm but my usage is basic so I don’t need it and stopped using it
